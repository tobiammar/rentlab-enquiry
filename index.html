How Inquiries work?

Step 1

Identify your desired property
Consider what you need in a property, including location, size, and any specific amenities.

Step 2

Fill out inquiry form
Complete the form with your contact details and the characteristics of the property you want.

Step 3

Wait for a Response
Keep an eye out for a response from the landlord. They will send a proposal that matches your inquiry via email.
